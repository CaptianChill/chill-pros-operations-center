Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 22, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how Chill Professional LLC, doing business as Chill Pros, collects, uses, stores, and shares information through the Chill Pros Operations Center and its connected services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect customer and business contact information, service addresses, equipment information, model and serial numbers, job details, technician notes, photographs, scheduling information, quotes, invoices, payment status, user-account information, and technical logs.

3. Jobber Integration Data

When a user authorizes Jobber, the application may access and synchronize authorized Jobber data such as clients, properties, jobs, visits, quotes, invoices, and related status information. Access is limited to the permissions approved during authorization.

4. How Information Is Used

Information is used to deliver field-service operations, manage customers and equipment, schedule and document work, prepare quotes and invoices, coordinate technicians, support customer service, maintain records, improve the application, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Sharing

Information may be shared with authorized Chill Pros personnel and service providers that support hosting, authentication, storage, communications, payments, analytics, and business integrations. We do not sell personal information.

6. Data Storage and Security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards are used to protect information. Authentication credentials, payment details, and third-party access tokens should be handled through secured providers and backend systems rather than stored in public source code.

7. Data Retention

Information is retained as reasonably necessary for service history, accounting, warranty, safety, legal, and business purposes. Records may be deleted or anonymized when no longer required, subject to applicable obligations.

8. User Choices

Authorized users may disconnect third-party integrations, request correction of inaccurate information, or request deletion where legally permitted. Some records may need to be retained for accounting, contractual, warranty, or legal reasons.

9. Children

The application is intended for business use and is not directed to children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Policy may be updated as features, integrations, or legal requirements change. The effective date above will be revised when material updates are made.
